Welcome to Mary's Toys, where you can find the best toys for your child's imagination!

Our store is founded and owned by Mary, a passionate toy enthusiast who has dedicated her life to making children's dreams come true. Mary has been in the toy industry for over 20 years, starting as a sales associate and working her way up to store manager before finally opening her own toy shop.

Her passion for toys started at a young age, where she spent countless hours playing with her own toy collection and creating imaginary worlds. Mary's knowledge of toys is vast, and she takes pride in hand-selecting each item that is sold in her store.

From classic toys that have stood the test of time to the latest and greatest in technology, Mary's Toy Shop has something for every child. But what sets Mary apart is her dedication to her customers. She believes in providing exceptional customer service and takes the time to get to know each and every one of her customers. Mary's Toy Shop is more than just a store; it's a community where families can come to find the perfect toy and make lasting memories.

Mary is also committed to giving back to the community. She regularly donates toys to local children's hospitals and schools, and participates in toy drives during the holiday season. Thank you for choosing Mary's Toy Shop for all your toy needs. We are proud to be a part of your child's journey and can't wait to see what adventures lie ahead!
