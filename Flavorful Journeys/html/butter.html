Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a popular Indian dish made with tender chicken in a creamy tomato-based sauce, seasoned with herbs and spices, and finished with cream and butter. It's loved for its delicious taste and comforting qualities.

Creamy and Dreamy Butter Chicken


Preparation time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 30 minutes


Butter chicken is a classic Indian dish that's loved for its rich and creamy tomato-based sauce and delicious blend of spices.

This recipe takes that classic flavor and puts a fun twist on it, with a bit of sweetness and a touch of heat. It's perfect for a cozy night in or for serving at your next dinner party.

Instruction

Heat up the butter and vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook for 3-4 minutes, until it becomes soft and translucent.

Add the minced garlic, grated ginger, garam masala, turmeric, paprika, cumin, and cayenne pepper to the pan. Stir everything together and cook for another 2-3 minutes, until the spices are fragrant.

Add the diced tomatoes to the pan and stir everything together. Allow the mixture to simmer for about 10 minutes.

Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, puree the tomato mixture until it is smooth.

Add the chicken pieces to the pan and stir everything together to coat the chicken in the sauce. Cover the pan and cook for about 15 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.

Add the heavy cream and honey to the pan and stir everything together. Allow the mixture to simmer for another 5-10 minutes, until it has thickened and the chicken is well coated in the sauce.

Season the dish with salt and pepper to taste. Serve the butter chicken hot, garnished with chopped cilantro.

Ingredients


  • boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces 1 pound
  • Onion, chopped 1
  • Garlic cloves, minced 2
  • Grated ginger 1 tablespoon
  • Vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
  • butter 2 tablespoons
  • garam masala 1 tablespoon
  • turmeric 1 tablespoon
  • paprika 1 tablespoon
  • cumin 1/2 teaspoon
  • cayenne pepper 1/4 teaspoon
  • diced tomatoes 1 can
  • heavy cream 1 cup
  • honey 1/4 cup
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped for garnish
