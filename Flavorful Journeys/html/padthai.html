Pad Thai

Pad Thai is a popular stir-fried noodle dish from Thailand known for its sweet and savory flavors. Made with rice noodles, vegetables, tofu, and shrimp or chicken, it's typically seasoned with tamarind and fish sauce and topped with peanuts and lime wedges. It's a staple street food in Thailand and enjoyed worldwide for its delicious taste and easy preparation.

Easy-Peasy Pad Thai for Busy Weeknights


Preparation time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 15 minutes


Looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that's quick and easy to make? This Pad Thai recipe is just what you need! With its tangy and savory flavors and perfect balance of textures, this dish is sure to please everyone at the dinner table.

Best of all, it takes only a few ingredients and minimal prep time, making it a perfect choice for busy weeknights.

Instruction

Start by soaking the rice noodles in warm water for about 10 minutes, or until they are soft and pliable. Drain the noodles and set them aside.

In a small mixing bowl, combine the tamarind paste, fish sauce, brown sugar, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes. Mix well to make the Pad Thai sauce.

In a large pan or wok, heat up the vegetable oil over high heat. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.

Add the shrimp to the pan and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they turn pink and are cooked through. Remove the shrimp from the pan and set them aside.

Add the sliced red bell pepper to the pan and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until they are tender-crisp.

Add the cooked rice noodles to the pan, along with the Pad Thai sauce. Toss everything together until the noodles are coated with the sauce and heated through.

Add the cooked shrimp, bean sprouts, and sliced green onions to the pan, and stir-fry for an additional minute or so until everything is well combined.

Serve the Pad Thai hot, garnished with chopped peanuts, fresh cilantro leaves, and lime wedges.

Ingredients


  • Rice noodles 8 ounces
  • Vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic cloves, minced 2
  • Shrimp, peeled and deveined 1/2 pound
  • Red bell pepper, sliced 1
  • Bean sprouts 2 cups
  • Green onions, sliced 2
  • Roasted peanuts, chopped 1/4 cup
  • Fresh cilantro leaves 1/4 cup
  • Lime wedges for serving

For Pad Thai Sauce:

  • Tamarind paste 2 tablespoons
  • Fish sauce 2 tablespoons
  • Brown sugar 2 tablespoons
  • Soy sauce 1 tablespoon
  • Red pepper flakes 1/4 teaspoon
