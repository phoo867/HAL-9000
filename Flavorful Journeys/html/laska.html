Laska

Laksa is a popular spicy noodle soup that originated in Southeast Asia, particularly in Malaysia and Singapore, made with rice noodles in a rich, coconut milk-based curry broth, flavored with lemongrass and other spices. It's typically served with toppings such as chicken, shrimp, or vegetables, and garnished with bean sprouts, cilantro, and lime wedges.

Creamy and Spicy Laksa for a Flavorful Meal


Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 25 minutes


If you're in the mood for something spicy, creamy, and delicious, this Laksa recipe is just what you need!

Made with rice noodles in a rich and flavorful coconut milk-based broth, and loaded with a variety of toppings, this dish is sure to satisfy your cravings and leave you feeling satisfied. Best of all, it's easy to make and customizable to your liking.

Instruction

Start by cooking the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain the noodles and set them aside.

In a large pot, heat up the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, minced garlic, and grated ginger, and cook for 2-3 minutes until they are soft and fragrant.

Add the red curry paste to the pot and stir-fry for 1 minute until it is well combined with the onion mixture.

Pour in the chicken or vegetable broth, coconut milk, fish sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

While the broth is simmering, cook the chicken, shrimp, or tofu in a separate pan until they are cooked through. Set them aside.

Add the vegetables to the broth and let them cook for 2-3 minutes until they are tender.

Divide the cooked rice noodles into 4 serving bowls. Top the noodles with the cooked meat or tofu and ladle the hot broth and vegetables over the top.

Garnish the Laksa with fresh cilantro leaves and lime wedges.

Ingredients


  • Rice noodles 8 ounces
  • Vegetable oil 1 tablespoon
  • Onion, chopped 1
  • Garlic cloves, minced 2
  • Grated ginger 1 tablespoon
  • Red curry paste 2 tablespoons
  • Chicken or vegetable broth 4 cups
  • Coconut milk 1 can
  • Fish sauce 1 tablespoon
  • Brown sugar 1 tablespoon
  • Lime juice 1 tablespoon
  • Chicken, shrimp, or tofu, sliced 1/2 pound
  • Vegetables 2 cups
  • Fresh cilantro and lime wedges for garnish
