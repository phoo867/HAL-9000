Cinnamon Rolls

Indulge in a warm, gooey, and irresistible treat with our tantalizing cinnamon rolls. These soft and fluffy pastries are swirled with a buttery cinnamon-sugar filling, baked to golden perfection, and topped with a luscious cream cheese glaze. Get ready to experience a taste of pure comfort and happiness with every bite of these cinnamon-infused delights.

Cinnamon Rolls: Sweet Swirls of Happiness

Preparation Time: 2 hours
Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Get ready to embark on a cinnamon-filled adventure with the oh-so-delicious Cinnamon Rolls recipe.

Brace yourself for swirls of happiness, an aroma that'll make your kitchen feel like a cozy bakery, and a taste that will transport you to pure bliss.

Instruction

In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, yeast, and salt. Mix them together like a DJ spinning beats at a cinnamon party.

Add the melted butter, warm milk, eggs, and vanilla extract to the bowl. Get your hands in there and mix everything until a dough forms. It's like kneading your way to cinnamon paradise!

Transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface and knead it for a good 5-7 minutes. Imagine you're giving it a mini massage, making it smooth and elastic. Show that dough some love!

Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a towel, and let it rest in a cozy spot for about an hour. Watch it rise like a happy balloon, doubling in size and getting ready to delight your taste buds.

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with a generous amount of butter. That's the secret ingredient that adds an extra touch of magic to your cinnamon rolls!

Roll out the dough on a floured surface, creating a rectangle that's about ¼ inch thick. Smear the softened butter all over the surface, like you're giving it a buttery blanket. Sprinkle the brown sugar and ground cinnamon on top, creating a swirl of sweetness.

It's time to roll it up! Starting from one of the longer sides, carefully roll the dough into a log. Slice it into equal-sized pieces, about 1½ inches thick. Place each cinnamon roll into the greased baking dish, spacing them out like happy little cinnamon soldiers.

Pop the baking dish into the preheated oven and let those cinnamon rolls bake for 20-25 minutes, or until they turn a golden hue that will make your heart skip a beat. Resist the temptation to peek too soon—the magic is happening inside that oven!

While the cinnamon rolls are baking to perfection, let's whip up the cream cheese frosting. In a bowl, combine the softened cream cheese and butter. Blend them together like a friendship that's meant to be. Add powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and milk, and beat until creamy and smooth. Feel free to adjust the sweetness and consistency to your liking.

As the cinnamon rolls come out of the oven, let them cool for a few minutes. Spread that luscious cream cheese frosting on top, allowing it to melt and ooze into every nook and cranny.

Now, behold the masterpiece you've created! Take a moment to admire those golden swirls of cinnamon goodness, topped with a creamy layer of frosting. It's almost too beautiful to eat, but trust me, the taste is even more heavenly.

Grab a plate, choose your favorite cinnamon roll (or maybe two, no judgment here), and take a big bite. Let the flavors dance on your taste buds as the sweet cinnamon and buttery dough melt in your mouth. It's like a warm hug from a pastry.

Share the joy! Gather your loved ones, your friends, or anyone who appreciates a mouthwatering treat. Watch their faces light up with delight as they indulge in these homemade cinnamon rolls. It's a guaranteed crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients


For the dough:

  • All-purpose flour 4 cups
  • Granulated sugar 1/3 cup
  • Active dry yeast 2 1/4 teaspoons
  • Salt 1 teaspoon
  • Unsalted butter, melted 1/2 cup
  • Whole milk, warmed 1 cup
  • Large eggs 2
  • Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon

For the filling:

  • Unsalted butter, softened 1/2 cup
  • Brown sugar 1 cup packed
  • Ground cinnamon 2 tablespoons

For the cream cheese frosting:

  • Cream cheese, softened 4 ounces
  • Unsalted butter, softened 1/4 cup
  • Powdered sugar 2 cups
  • Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
  • Milk 2-3 tablespoons
