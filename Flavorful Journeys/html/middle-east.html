South-East Cuisine

Southeast Asian cuisine is a diverse culinary tradition known for bold and complex flavors that combine sweet, sour, salty, and spicy elements. It features rice, noodles, seafood, herbs, tropical fruits, and spices like lemongrass, ginger, garlic, and chilies. Popular dishes include pad Thai, pho, nasi goreng, and green curry.
