Jyuwari Soba

Jyuwari Soba is a type of Japanese soba noodle made entirely from buckwheat flour, making it a gluten-free alternative to traditional wheat-based noodles. It has a nutty flavor and a delicate texture, and is often served in soups or stir-fry dishes. Jyuwari Soba is a popular and nutritious choice for those with gluten sensitivities, as well as for anyone looking to try a unique and healthy type of noodle.

Spicy Jyuwari Soba Noodle Bowl


Preparation time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 15 minutes


Ready for a delicious gluten-free noodle bowl? Jyuwari Soba is made with 100% buckwheat flour and has a nutty flavor and delicate texture.

Add in some shiitake mushrooms, baby bok choy, and a spicy broth for a tasty dish that's out of this world. Let's get cooking!

Instruction

Let's kick off this culinary adventure by cooking the pasta according to the package instructions. Remember, we're aiming for that perfect al dente texture, so keep an eye on it!

In a medium-sized pot, add 4 cups of water and bring it to a boil. Add the soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar, grated ginger, minced garlic, and red pepper flakes. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes.

While the broth is simmering, let's prepare the toppings. Slice the green onions, chop the cilantro, and slice the shiitake mushrooms and baby bok choy.

In a separate pan, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the shiitake mushrooms and bok choy and cook for 5 minutes until they're tender.

Once the broth is ready, season it with salt and pepper to taste.

To serve, divide the cooked Jyuwari Soba noodles among four bowls, then ladle the hot broth over the noodles.

Arrange the prepared toppings on top of the noodles and broth.

Squeeze lime wedges over each bowl for added tanginess, and enjoy your spicy Jyuwari Soba noodle bowl!

Ingredients

  • Jyuwari Soba noodles 8 ounces
  • Water 4 cups
  • Soy sauce 1/4 cup
  • Mirin 1/4 cup
  • Brown sugar 2 tablespoons
  • Salt and black pepper To taste
  • Grated ginger 1 tablespoon
  • Garlic cloves, minced 2
  • Red pepper flakes 1/2 teaspoon
  • Green onions 2
  • Chopped cilantro 1/2 cup
  • Sliced shiitake mushrooms 1 cup
  • Baby bok choy, sliced 1 cup
  • Vegetable oil 2 tablespoons
  • Lime wedges for serving
