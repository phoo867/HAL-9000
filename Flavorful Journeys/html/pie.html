Pumpkin Pie

Indulge in the warm, comforting flavors of autumn with a slice of delicious pumpkin pie. With its flaky crust and smooth, spiced pumpkin filling, this classic dessert captures the essence of fall in every bite.

Pumpkin Perfection: The Ultimate Fall Delight

Preparation Time: 30 minutes
Cooking Time: 50 minutes

Hello, Hello! With my homemade pumpkin pie recipe, you can elevate your fall recipes to a whole new level. Say goodbye to store-bought options and get ready to indulge in the creamy delight of fresh, homemade pumpkin puree that will add a burst of flavor to your seasonal dishes.

Let's roll up our sleeves and dive into the magic of creating pumpkin puree from scratch!

Instruction

Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Begin by making the pie crust. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the cold, cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to cut the butter into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.

Gradually add the ice water, a tablespoon at a time, and mix until the dough comes together. Shape the dough into a disk, wrap it in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

While the dough chills, prepare the pumpkin filling. In a separate bowl, whisk together the pumpkin puree, sugar, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, cloves, vanilla extract, eggs, and evaporated milk until well combined.

On a floured surface, roll out the chilled dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer the dough to a 9-inch pie dish, gently pressing it into the bottom and sides. Trim any excess dough and crimp the edges.

Pour the pumpkin filling into the prepared pie crust. Smooth the top with a spatula and place the pie in the preheated oven.

Bake for 15 minutes at 425°F (220°C) to set the crust, then reduce the oven temperature to 350°F (175°C). Continue baking for an additional 35-40 minutes or until the filling is set and the center is slightly jiggly but not soupy.

Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool completely on a wire rack. Once cooled, refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld and the pie to set.

Serve chilled or at room temperature, topped with a dollop of whipped cream. Enjoy the perfect balance of spiced sweetness in every mouthwatering bite of this homemade pumpkin pie!

Ingredients


For the dough:

  • All-purpose flour 1 ½ cups
  • Ice water 1/4 cup
  • Unsalted butter ½ cupr, cold and cubed
  • Salt ½ teaspoon

For the filling:

  • Pumpkin puree 2 cups
  • Granulated sugar 1 cup
  • Ground cinnamon 1 tablespoon
  • Ground ginger 1/2 teaspoon
  • Ground nutmeg 1/4 teaspoon
  • Ground cloves 1/4 teaspoon
  • Vanilla extract 1/2 teaspoon
  • Large eggs 3
  • Evaporated milk 1 cup

Additional:

  • Whipped cream, for serving
