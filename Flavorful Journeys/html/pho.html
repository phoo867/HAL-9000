Pho

Pho is a traditional Vietnamese noodle soup that is known for its rich and savory broth, thin rice noodles, and flavorful meat toppings such as beef, chicken, or shrimp. The soup is typically seasoned with herbs and spices such as cinnamon, star anise, and coriander, and is served with a variety of garnishes such as bean sprouts, basil, and lime wedges. Pho is a popular dish in Vietnam and has gained worldwide popularity for its delicious taste and comforting qualities.

Easy-Peasy Pho for a Cozy Night In


Preparation time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 20 minutes


Looking for a delicious and satisfying meal that's quick and easy to make? This Pad Thai recipe is just what you need! With its tangy and savory flavors and perfect balance of textures, this dish is sure to please everyone at the dinner table.

Best of all, it takes only a few ingredients and minimal prep time, making it a perfect choice for busy weeknights.

Instruction

In a large pot, bring the beef or chicken broth to a boil. Add the cinnamon sticks, star anise pods, coriander seeds, and fennel seeds to the pot, and let them simmer for 10 minutes to infuse the broth with flavor.

While the broth is simmering, cook the rice noodles according to the package instructions. Drain the noodles and set them aside.

In a separate pot, cook the beef or chicken over medium-high heat for 2-3 minutes until they are browned and cooked through. Remove the meat from the pot and set it aside.

Divide the cooked rice noodles into 4 serving bowls. Top the noodles with the cooked meat, bean sprouts, basil leaves, and cilantro leaves.

Once the broth is ready, strain it through a sieve to remove the spices. Pour the hot broth over the noodles and toppings in each bowl.

Serve the Pho hot, with a lime wedge, hoisin sauce, and Sriracha sauce on the side.

Ingredients


  • Beef or chicken broth 8 cups
  • Cinnamon sticks 2
  • Star anise pods 2
  • Coriander seeds 1 tablespoon
  • Fennel seeds 1 tablespoon
  • Bean sprouts 2 cups
  • Beef sirloin or chicken breast, sliced thinly 1/2 pound
  • Rice noodles 8 ounces
  • Fresh basil leaves 1/2 cup
  • Fresh cilantro leaves 1/2 cup
  • Lime 1, cut into wedges
  • Hoisin sauce and Sriracha sauce for serving
