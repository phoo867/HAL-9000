Pasta

Pasta, the timeless culinary delight loved by millions around the globe, is a true masterpiece of simplicity and versatility. Whether you prefer it al dente with a classic marinara sauce or toss it with an assortment of fresh vegetables and herbs, pasta never fails to satisfy and captivate the senses.

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Join me on a flavorful journey with my Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta recipe.

Experience the luscious blend of creamy sauce, aromatic garlic, and irresistible Parmesan cheese in this delightful dish. Get ready to indulge in pasta perfection with a touch of creativity and a whole lot of flavor!

Instruction

Let's kick off this culinary adventure by cooking the pasta according to the package instructions. Remember, we're aiming for that perfect al dente texture, so keep an eye on it!

While the pasta is doing its thing, grab a skillet and melt the butter over medium heat. Now, let's add the minced garlic and watch the magic happen as it sizzles and fills the kitchen with its irresistible aroma. Ah, heavenly!

Time to add the heavy cream and let it work its creamy wonders. We want it to simmer gently, so keep the heat at a cozy medium. Give it a few minutes to thicken slightly, and take a moment to appreciate how the kitchen transforms into a delicious sanctuary of flavors.

Now, let's take our grated Parmesan cheese and sprinkle it into the skillet, little by little. Stir it like there's no tomorrow, making sure it melts into the creamy sauce, creating a symphony of gooey perfection. Oh, the cheesy goodness!

Seasoning time! Sprinkle some salt and black pepper into the mix, adding just the right amount of zing to elevate the flavors. Don't be shy hereâ€”taste, adjust, and let your palate be the guide to pasta paradise.

Our pasta is ready to join the party! Toss it gently into the skillet, making sure each strand is coated in that luxurious creamy sauce. Watch as the pasta absorbs all the flavors, turning into a divine masterpiece of deliciousness.

It's showtime! Remove the skillet from the heat and get ready to add that final touch. Sprinkle some freshly chopped parsley on top for a burst of vibrant color and a hint of freshness. The dish is now dressed to impress!

Time to dig in! Serve this creamy garlic Parmesan pasta straight from the skillet onto plates, and watch everyone's eyes widen with anticipation. For an extra touch of indulgence, sprinkle some extra grated Parmesan cheese on top. Go ahead, embrace the cheese lover within!

Ingredients

  • Pasta (spaghetti or fettuccine) 8 ounces
  • Butter 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic 4 cloves, minced
  • Heavy cream 1 cup
  • Parmesan cheese 1 cup, grated
  • Salt and black pepper To taste
  • Fresh parsley chopped (for garnish)
