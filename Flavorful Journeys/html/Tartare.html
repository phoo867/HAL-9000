Steak Tartare

Prepare your taste buds for an adventurous culinary delight with Steak Tartare, a dish that showcases the raw beauty of high-quality beef. This classic French creation combines finely chopped raw beef, tangy condiments, and aromatic herbs to create a symphony of flavors and textures. With each bite, you'll experience a harmonious balance of richness, acidity, and freshness that will leave you craving more.

Raw Perfection: Steak Tartare

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Welcome to the world of culinary journey with my tantalizing recipe for Steak Tartare.

Get ready to embark on a journey of raw perfection as we combine the finest ingredients to create a dish that will leave you begging for more.

Instruction

It's all about the beef, baby! Make sure you have a high-quality beef tenderloin. Using a sharp knife, finely chop the beef into small, even pieces. We're aiming for a texture that's tender and melt-in-your-mouth.

In a bowl, mix together the Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, capers, red onion, fresh parsley, and chives. This vibrant medley of flavors will elevate the beef to new heights of deliciousness. Feel free to add a dash of hot sauce if you're feeling daring!

Time to bring it all together! Add the finely chopped beef to the bowl of condiments. Drizzle in the extra-virgin olive oil and season with salt and black pepper. Now, it's your time to shine! Mix everything gently but thoroughly, ensuring the beef is well coated with the flavorful condiments.

It's plating time! Grab a ring mold or a round cookie cutter and place it on a plate. Fill it with the prepared Steak Tartare mixture, gently pressing it down to create a neat, round shape. Carefully remove the mold and admire your culinary masterpiece.

Let's add some flair! Surround the Steak Tartare with a handful of mixed greens or microgreens, providing a vibrant pop of color and freshness. It's all about that visually stunning presentation!

Grab those toasted baguette slices or crackers, and get ready to dive in. Each bite is a textural marvelâ€”creamy, meaty, and bursting with flavor. Spread the Steak Tartare onto the bread or crackers, allowing the different elements to dance on your palate.

Close your eyes, savor the raw perfection, and let the flavors transport you to a gastronomic paradise. Share this unique culinary experience with fellow adventurous food enthusiasts, and let the joy of Steak Tartare bring smiles to your faces.

Ingredients

  • High-quality beef tenderloin 1 pound. finely chopped
  • Dijon mustard 2 tablespoons
  • Worcestershire sauce 1 tablespoon
  • Capers 2 tablespoons, drained and finely chopped
  • Red onion 2 tablespoons, finely chopped
  • Fresh parsley, 2 tablespoons, finely chopped
  • Fresh chives 1 tablespoon, finely chopped
  • Extra-virgin olive oil 1 tablespoon
  • Hot sauce (optional) 1 teaspoon
  • Salt and black pepper To Taste
  • Toasted baguette slices or crackers (for serving)
  • Mixed greens or microgreens for garnish
