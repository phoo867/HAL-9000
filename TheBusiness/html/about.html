Alexis

Alexis, CEO of Gamework Studio, co-founded the company with a mission to create innovative and immersive games. She fosters a culture of creativity and technology, resulting in numerous awards and accolades. Alexis is committed to diversity and inclusion in the tech industry and continues to lead Gamework Studio with passion and dedication.

Phoo

Phoo, a passionate gamer since childhood, studied computer science and worked for a game development company. But she wanted to create games that reflected her vision. So, she started her own studio, GameWorks Studio. As the COO, Phoo led a small team of game developers, working hard to create innovative games. GameWorks Studio's first game was a hit, and investors and publishers soon came knocking. Today, GameWorks Studio is a successful studio pushing the boundaries of game development, led by Phoo, a dedicated and talented female COO.
